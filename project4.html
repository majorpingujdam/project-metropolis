The Vessel

The Vessel is a striking centerpiece of Hudson Yards in New York City.
Designed by British designer Thomas Heatherwick, it consists of a series of interconnected staircases offering unique views of the city.
Since its completion, The Vessel has become a popular attraction, symbolizing the fusion of modern architecture and urban exploration.



The Future of The Vessel:
The Vessel is expected to evolve as a hub for art, culture, and technology in the coming years.
With potential integrations of digital experiences and artistic collaborations, it could become a key venue for interactive exhibits and events.
However, I would like to imagine it in a war time, setting in an apocalyptic background, because the future is not always bright for the human race.
